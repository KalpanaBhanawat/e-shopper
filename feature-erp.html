An ERP, virtual assistant and a transparency, accountability monitor   

  • HR

    HR module comes up with power pack feature like, Employee attendance management, and their movements (when they login\logout  from the system). Create departments, posts, employee, affiliates and extract all their information in one click whenever you need. Manage who can view/edit/delete what, give access control writes to your employees.  

  • Digital Marketing

    Create leads, convert them into opportunities. Save all the conversation you had with them (E-mail, telephone, SMS) and see history of conversation in just-in-time manner. Design and save newsletters, social posts, blog posts, SMS and schedule them to send on a particular date and time.

  • Accounts

    Manage your accounting in a professional and the double book entry system based accounting system. Human readable accounting, which creates technical reports in a go. Send account statements to any party in just one click. Automatic calculation of exchange profit/loss, per invoice based.


  • Communication

    Strong centralized E-Mail management system. Multiple E-Mail account management. Separately filter E-Mail communication with any customer in just one click to have a communication history. Post based permission for employees. 


  • Projects

    Create a project, assign a team to it, assign tasks to team members. Set deadline, estimate time. Analyze which employee is doing what in real time. See how long a task is being performed, which employee is in the  red zone and in the green zone. Have an environment where there is full transparency.


  • Production

    Create job cards, process and forward them to the next department, Have a sharp eye on flow of jobs from one department to another. Dispatch with dispatch Challan, E-Mail customer about the delivery of the product and include a tracking URL.


  • Store

    Multi warehouse management, add stock transactions, items.
